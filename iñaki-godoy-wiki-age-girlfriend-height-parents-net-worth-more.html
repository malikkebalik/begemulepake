How Inaki Godoy became an actor

Iñaki Godoy’s path to becoming an actor is a testament to his unwavering dedication to the craft.

From a young age, he displayed a passion for performing, taking his first steps onto the stage at the tender age of 5 with the Stage Company of the Independent Musical Theater.

He continued to nurture his talents through specialized education in acting, singing, and dancing.

But Iñaki’s quest for artistic excellence didn’t stop there. To further refine his skills, he enrolled in courses at Hollywood Stunt and Drama during the summers, determined to reach the pinnacle of his craft.

In 2016, Iñaki Godoy achieved a significant breakthrough by landing his first role in the TV series “Blue Demon.” This marked the beginning of his ascent in the entertainment industry.

Soon after, he made his presence felt in the Mexican TV series “La querida del Centauro,” garnering attention and acclaim. Although fame initially radiated primarily within his hometown, Iñaki harbored ambitions far grander than that.

He persisted, taking incremental steps in his career, participating in series such as “Por la Máscara: La Serie Web,” “Sin miedo a la verdad,” and “Los elegidos.”

As his star continued to rise, Iñaki Godoy earned the opportunity to shine on the big screen with the film “Go Youth!” and the series “Who Killed Sara?”

“Who Killed Sara?” was a turning point for Iñaki, marking his debut collaboration with Netflix. This partnership would prove to be highly fruitful, as he went on to sign back-to-back series with the streaming giant.

In “The Imperfects,” he portrayed Juan Ruiz opposite Italia Ricci Morgan and Taylor Campbell, showcasing his versatility and talent.

Iñaki Godoy has exciting projects in the pipeline, including the movies “MexZombies” and “No Abras La Puerta.”
