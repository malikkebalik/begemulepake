Jason Thielges Died Unexpectedly. Did He Attempt Suicide?

August 20, 2024
blog

A tragic incident happened; the resident of Moorhead City, Minnesota, Jason Thielges, passed away on 24 October 2023. Jason was a coach of football at Davies High School, Fargo, North Dakota. The community of Jason, including his family and students, is suffering from a mourning situation.

Our condolences and sympathy are with Jason and his loved ones who care for him and love to spend time with him.

Who was Jason Thielges?

Jason Thielges was well-known in the tight-knit town for his good attitude, energetic football coach at Fargo Davies High School, musical skills, and kind-hearted personality.
Jason was an under-50. He was actively involved in athletics and a role model for his students, who inspired them often. His successful influence on young athletes had a positive impact on them, which assisted them not only on the playing field but also in the field of life.

Many people are giving tribute to Jason and announcing the news of his death around the community. The Fargo Davies High School is providing students with mental health support who are close to Jason, which had a massive impact on them after Jason’s death.

Shannon Borke VanHorn wrote,

“It’s never easy when a former student passes away. Prayers to Jason Thielges’ family, and to all his Viking brothers from VCSU. They were some of my first students when I came to VCSU. The mental health of students has been discussed a lot post covid, but the mental health of teachers and coaches also needs to be addressed. R.I.P., Jason.”

What happened with Jason Thielges?

Authorities in North Dakota continue to investigate Jason’s case, but according to the sources, he attempted suicide. After knowing of Jason’s suicide attempts, his death became a mystery for the entire community, and many of his well-known relatives refused to believe that he had committed suicide. Because no one knew about his mental health issues at the time, he struggled.

Any individual with a mental health condition needs to talk to someone or consider meeting with a therapist.

When will Jason Thielges’ funeral be held?

Jason’s family and the whole community are suffering from a tragic situation where they lost their beloved part of their family from their life; the family of Jason has not yet disclosed the details of Jason’s funeral. Maybe they need time to recover from this trauma; we have to respect their decision and hope that they will disclose it soon.

Our condolence and support are to Jason and his family, who are suffering from this terrible time. May God open the door of Heaven for Jason and give his family patience and power to recover from this trauma.
