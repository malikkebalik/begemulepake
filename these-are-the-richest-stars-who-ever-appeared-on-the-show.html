The Voice has helped many contestants get their start in the music industry or helped further an existing musical career. Let’s take a look at the success of those who made it to the show.

Here are the richest stars who ever appeared on The Voice, as well as a few honorable mentions, according to research from Celebrity Net Worth and information around the web.

Jason “Sundance” Head

  • Net worth: $450,000

Jason “Sundance” Head won season 11 of The Voice. Billy Gilman was the runner-up. His first album, 2016 & Gruene, was released March 2012. He then followed up with his second album, Soul Country. His 2016 single, “Darlin’ Don’t Go,” reached the No. 4 spot on the U.S. country music charts.
