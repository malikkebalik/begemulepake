Who is Rex Linn?

Born on November 13, 1956, Rex Linn is an American film and television actor who is best known for playing the role of Sgt. Frank Tripp in the CBS drama CSI: Miami. 

He is also famous for playing Kevin Wachtell in the television series Better Call Saul and for the role of Richard Travers in the 1993 action thriller film Cliffhanger.

Linn was born to Darlene (née Deere) and James Paul Linn in Spearman, Texas. He was raised alongside his two other siblings.

He is the third child and second son of his parents.

His father, James Paul Linn, was a lawyer in Oklahoma City, so for his work purpose, their family hailed to Oklahoma in August 1969.

Rex went to Heritage Hall and later Casady School. Similarly, in 1980, he graduated from Oklahoma State University.

Lin’s acting career in the film industry began with the film Dark Before Dawn, produced by his best friend, Edward K. Gaylord II.

Before landing on his first movie, he served as vice president of energy lending for the Lakeshore Bank. He also worked in an oil company.

Similarly, during his auditioning phase, he appeared in various minor roles in commercials and other projects.
