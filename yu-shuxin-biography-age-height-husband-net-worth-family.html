Esther Yu (Chinese: 虞书欣, born December 18, 1995), also known as Yu Shuxin, is a Chinese actress and singer. After making her acting debut in the 2016 drama Border Town Prodigal, she gained recognition for her roles in the hit television series The Advisors Alliance (2017) and Find Yourself (2020).
