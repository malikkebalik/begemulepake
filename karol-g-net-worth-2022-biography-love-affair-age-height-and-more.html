Meaning of Karol G’s name

Karol G, the pseudonym by which this artist is known, originates from a combination of her first names.

Childhood, Youth and Family Life by Karol G

Karol G is the daughter of Martha Cecilia Navarro and Guillermo Giraldo, who is also her manager. She has two sisters: Verónica Giraldo, the oldest; and Jessica Giraldo, the youngest of the family. From an early age, Karol G showed a true passion for music and singing; since then she began to sing and write songs.

At the age of five, she entered a music course with her sister, Jessica Giraldo. Her parents always supported her, to the point that they enrolled her, along with her little sister Jessica, in every music course they could find. Her greatest desire has always been to sing, express her views and entertain people with her music.

In addition to her natural talent for music, Karol G studied music at the University of Antioquia. Then, for three months, she studied marketing in New York, United States; but she gave up that career for music.

Beginnings of Karol G in Music

Her beginnings in music occurred from a very young age; however, she became known when she was a participant in the television contest called El Factor Xs Colombia (2006), where she showed her musical talent.

Around that same time, she managed to sign a contract with Mauricio Pabón, manager of Flamingo Records Colombia. In the beginning, she also met J Balvin, when she appeared at a 15-year party of a cousin of hers; there they sang together.

Karol G’s musical genre

Karol G is an unpredictable and adventurous artist who knows how to navigate between various musical genres such as reggaeton, trap, rap, hip-hop, rock, dance, romantics, and merengue rhythms.

Trajectory and Legacy of Karol G

In 2010 Karol G became part of the musical team of the artist, also Colombian, Reykon; Thus began in the world of reggaeton, as the singer’s showgirl.

In 2012 she debuted with her hit “301”, along with Reykon, which managed to position himself at number one on many television channels and radio stations. The following year Karol G released “Amor de dos”, together with Puerto Rican artist Nicky Jam. This piece became a success, and the corresponding video, released on September 28 of the same year, exceeds 20 million views on its official YouTube channel.

In 2014, Karol G premiered the song “Ricos Besos”; A theme with which she achieved more direct communication with her fans, who chanted the lyrics at each concert she gave.

The following year the artist released her own adaptation of the song “Mil horas”, by Andrés Calamaro, entitled “Ya no te creo”; with moderate success on Colombian radio stations. Sometime later her single I want to do it came out, along with De La Ghetto.

In 2016 Karol G released the songs “Casi nada”, “Muñeco de Lego” and “Hello”; the latter with the Puerto Rican singer Ozuna.

Some you get what you want, others don’t, but you always win. There is nothing that stops me. I am willing to overcome and enjoy whatever comes. It is my law, it is my norm. In 2018 Karol G obtained the nomination for the Billboard Latin Music Awards in the category Top Latin Albums Female Artist of the Year. The album was promoted with a musical tour called Unstoppable Tour, through which she gave concerts in the United States, Canada, and Latin America.

On March 10, 2018, she released the single “Pineapple”; Besides her, Alejandro “Sky” Ramírez participated in the production. The song was released together with the corresponding official video clip. Later, she recorded the single “Tu pum pum”, with the collaboration of Shaggy, El Capitaan, and Sekuence.
