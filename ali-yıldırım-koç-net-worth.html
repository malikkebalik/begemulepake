What is Yildirim Ali Koc's Net Worth?

Yildirim Ali Koc is a Turkish businessman who has a net worth of $1 billion. Yildirim Ali Koc is the youngest third generation scion of the Koc family that runs Turkey's largest group of companies, Koc Holding. He restored his place among billionaires as the stock price of Koc Holding had increased by 30% throughout the past year. After finishing high school at Harrow School, London, he went on to earn his B.A. in Managerial Studies from Rice University, Houston, Texas, which was followed by taking a Master's degree from Harvard University, Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Upon graduation, he spent some time working for various companies in the States before getting back to Turkey to join the family business. Initially, he served as the Chief Executive of the information technologies group of the holding, whereas now he is the Chairman of the Board at Ford Otosan. In 2012, he acquired that post filling the shoes of his father Mustafa who had been at the helm for 40 years. Married to Nevbahar Demirağ, Yildirim Ali Koc is also a father of two.
