The Riverdale star has won the hearts of millions through Cole Sprouse’s phenomenal performance as Jughead Jones, the son of the town’s criminal gang, Southside Serpents, F. P. Jones. However, the fans still recognize him as a talented child actor. His memorable roles in movies like Big Daddy alongside Adam Sandler and the mega-hit NBC sitcom Friends, where he played Ross’s seven-year-old son Ben, earned him momentous popularity and recognition.

Saying so, his very recent confession about losing his virginity has taken social media, especially Twitter by storm. In Wednesday’s podcast episode of Call Her Daddy with Alex Cooper, Sprouse spilled the beans on his past relationships along with the revelation about losing his virginity at 14 on a family trip to Florida.

It was the same time when the 2005 sitcom The Suite Life of Zack & Cody was on air ,where the 30-year-old actor played the titular Cody Martin. Speaking in detail about the subject, he said:

“I was on a family trip in Florida, and I met this girl who was older, so that’s already dubious. … The first night we kind of made out, the second night — this makes me nervous, it’s so cringey — I, like, knocked on her hotel door. We were down at the beach on some chaise lounges and I looked at her, my heart was beating, and I finally mustered up the courage to deliver a line that my brother has never, ever let down from me. I looked around and I was like, ‘So, are you DTF?’ She goes, ‘What?’ And I go, ‘You know, down to f**k?’ I was 14. She looked at me and she was like, ‘Sure.'”

Cole also confessed about calling his brother Dylan that night as he required the room all to himself. “My brother just looks at me and he goes, ‘What the f**k are we supposed to do?’ I looked at him and I said another line that he’s never forgiven me for. I go, ‘I don’t know, go play chess or something,” he reminisced about lying to his brother.

Moving on to the subject of sex, Sprouse opened up about how it “lasted about 20 seconds” after which he did not remain in touch with the girl.

Later, he also talked about how the experience led him to take a more sexual approach to his future relationships ending in multiple breakups, and that all his exes would probably view their relationship to have had a “stronger sexual foundation than an emotional foundation.”
